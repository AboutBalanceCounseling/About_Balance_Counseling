Welcome to About Balance Counseling

Are you struggling with anxiety or depression? Do daily pressures from work, school, or relationships feel overwhelming? You are not alone.

David Ejchorszt, LCSW, has over 10 years of experience helping adults, teens, and couples overcome emotional challenges and regain a true sense of happiness and belonging. Using a personalized Cognitive Behavioral Approach, David works as your partner to identify goals and remove the barriers keeping you from living your best life.

In-person sessions in Longmont and secure online therapy across Colorado. My focus is always you.
